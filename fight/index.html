First Platformer

  • Originality: It is original work but pulls the basic aspects and mechanics from many traditional and classical games.
  • Technical Merit: Fluid animation and live tracking of health values. Both players can simultaniously play without input delay or lag between player characters.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned the basics of animation manipulation as well as keyboard binding. I learned how to properly setup animation cycles to avoid overriding previous animations or overlapping animations. The prototype allowed me to gate health loss per attack instead of being a constant update cycle and also understand collision detection in Phaser. I had the hardest time debugging as there were many more aspects to test between updates than the previous game. I had spent quite a long time trying to fix animation cycles overlapping because of this. The easiest part was getting the initial loading and playing of basic animations without interaction.
