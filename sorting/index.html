  • Player Experience Goals: A quick action based sorting game.
  • Gameplay: Controls: Arrow Keys This game is a sorting game where a player should match the color of the mail with the color of the mailbox by hitting the arrow keys pointing towards the colored mailbox that matches the color of the mail. Players lose health if they hit the wrong arrow keys, and are timed. On the loss of all HP points, players are told how many points they scored in the time played and are allowed to restart if they wish. Originally I wanted to create two different game states with different rules, but could not for times sake.
